Electronic Election FAQ



Q: Where do I cast my vote?

A: This year, we will be adapting electronic voting. We will send you the link to the website via email


Q: How do I vote?

A: You will need to click on the link and use the unique username and password we created for you to login to the website, view the ballot and vote


Q: Can I create my own username and password?

A: No. The Nomelec committee will create and assign a unique username and password for each voter to prevent others from creating an unauthorized account for self or others thus preventing "flying votes".


Q: How do I know if my ballot was sent or counted?

A: You will receive an automatic confirmation message that says "This form has been successfully submitted" immediately after you click submit. The Admin User (a designated Nomelec Co-chair) will be able to view in real time, submissions with the voter's name affiliated with the ballot.


Q: Is my ballot confidential?

A: Yes. Your ballot is only accessible to the designated Nomelec Admin User who will regularly monitor the website for smooth operation and troubleshoot glitches


Q: How do I know if my vote was counted?

A: Once you successfully submit the ballot, it will automatically update the website with your personal info and your vote. At the end of the election, we will provide all Nomelec committee members and independent checkers access as an Admin User to verify each ballot and votes.


Q: Can anyone alter the notes or results?

A: No. The Webmaster provides assurance that any information related to the result and outcome of this electronic voting can bot be tampered or altered.
